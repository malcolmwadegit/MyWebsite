About Me

An analyst and programmer moonlighting as a chef and barrista.

I'm an analyst at heart who is constantly intrigued by data and optimization. I have a constantly evolving poker data base that allows me to utilize data visualization tools to take a deeper look into my poker game and helps me easily identify holes in my performance that I can improve.

Living in the days of digital POS systems, we are constantly collecting data and the restaurant industry has the most to gain from this improvement. Being able to improve the processes and provide data driven solutions has been one of the most rewarding experiences in the past two years. Being lucky enough to work in multiple industries as an analyst, I've developed a mindset that with any problem can come a solution if you work hard enough to find one.
